We Resist

3D strategy-management game.

Unreal Engine/C++

Gameplay features

  • You manage your group of soldiers and resources to complete missions in a turn based setiing.
  • Map consist of regions. Each region tracks reputation of your soldiers based on passed missions and choices.
  • In each region you can build and manage a camp. You can assign soldiers on various camp roles (e.g. cook = less food needed per day), track their morale and stats. You can move soldiers between camps to optimize the travel distances to missions.
  • There is a variety of mission types (random, story, recruitment, etc.). Missions calculate the success probability based on number of assigned soldiers, their traits and stats.
  • Random events can trigger at various times (traveling to mission, on round end, etc.).

Srot

Inventory system demo.

Odin/raylib

  • Custom immidiate mode UI.
  • Item management. Drag and drop/click and drag, shortcuts for fast moving items between tabs.
  • Item sorting. Items in a tab can be sorted either by default (best fit), their type (armour, weapon, currency, ...), or by their stats.
  • Item selection. Multiple items can be grouped and moved around. You can destroy multiple items together or move them to a different tab.
  • Selected items can also be sorted as a group.
  • Items that are stackable can be split into two.
  • You can set an item type as an affinity of a tab. Then, on quick transfer, the item will move to a tab with associated affinity rather than to the opened one.
  • You can add a custom item with desired stats, or generate a random one.
